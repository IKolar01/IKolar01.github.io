About Me

Hi, Im Ian, I am currently a design student at Western Washington University. I am passionate about all things design and I am working towards my design bachalor of arts degree, and I will graduate in 2024. As a designer, I am committed to producing high-quality work that not only meets client needs but also communicates a meaningful message. I believe that good design is not just about aesthetics but also about solving problems and improving user experiences.

I have developed a strong foundation in design principals and software, such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Aftereffects, and Figma. I also have experience with varous types of printing, including screenprinting. My design intrests extend to branding, packaging, along with some web design.
